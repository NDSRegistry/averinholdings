Participation Unit Framework

Averin Holdings operates using a participation unit system designed for Roblox-native organizational structures.

Unit Structure

Participation units represent fractional operational interest in Averin Holdings activities. Each unit is valued at 2 Robux and provides holders with:

  • Proportional participation in governance decisions
  • Access to internal communications and reporting
  • Eligibility for operational roles and responsibilities
  • Recognition in organizational hierarchy systems

Important Disclaimers

Participation units are NOT securities or financial instruments. They are Roblox-native organizational mechanisms that:

  • Do not represent ownership or equity stakes
  • Do not entitle holders to profits or dividends
  • Cannot be traded on external markets
  • Are subject to Roblox Terms of Service
  • May be modified or discontinued at any time

Acquisition & Management

Participation units are issued through internal allocation processes to qualified participants. Units may be acquired through:

  • Direct allocation to staff and contributors
  • Recognition programs for sustained participation
  • Internal transfer mechanisms (subject to approval)
  • Periodic distribution events for active participants

Decision-Making Protocols

Strategic Initiatives

Authority: Board of Oversight
